Seaport continues to expand despite increasing threat of rising sea.

By: Maria Bonnemaison

Boston, MA -- The new and trency neighbordhood of Seaport was built on what was once an empty lot along the boston Harbor. Today you'll find brand new sky rises filled with corportate offices and luxury apartments, along with bustling construction sites scattered throughout the neighborhood.

Many development projects are wrapping up their construction, while others are preparing to being after receiving approval from the city. Seaport is still growing but so is the threat of flooding from rising sea-levels. What is now considered Boston's modern and affluent neighborhood is also most at risk to suffer the effects of climate change.
